AI Ping Pong










Developed by: Priyansh

Instructions

  
  1. Keep your Laptop screen straight
  2. Position yourself approximately 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop.
  3. Move your right wrist up and down. There should be a red dot on its location.
  4. Now press the play button and Position yourself 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop again.
  5. You control the red paddle. It will move as per the dot on your wrist.
